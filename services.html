GOVERNANCE

Good governance is essential to a well-functioning organization. I provide workshops and personal coaching for boards of directors and senior staff designed to help them improve their governance capacity.

NON-PROFIT MANAGEMENT

Non-profits have a fundamental fiduciary responsibility to it members and the community it serves. Effective and efficient management systems are critical to discharge this responsibility. I work with organizations to develop these systems, which include staff development, budgeting, information technology and operational planning and implementation.

STRATEGIC PLANNING

Strategic planning is the process of focusing an organization's future. I can assist to help visioning the future.

ANNUAL OPERATING PLAN DEVELOPMENT

Operational plans are the interpretation of an organization's strategic plan. It is where the rubber meets the road. I work with you to develop a process a process and format for constructing an operational plan for the organization.

STAFF DEVELOPMENT

Developing staff and involving them in the growth of the organization can be a challenging process. I can assist with designing staff involvement systems and help to avoid some of the pitfalls that may emerge.

ACCREDITATION

Preparing for an initial accreditation can be time consuming and even an onerous process for an organization. I work with organizations to assist in this preparation so that they experience full compliance with applicable standards.
